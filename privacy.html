Privacy Policy for Social Stack

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Social Stack. We are committed to protecting your business data and privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you connect your social media and marketing accounts (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp, and Google) through our platform.

2. Data Collection & API Usage

Our app functions as a management tool. We access the following via official APIs:

  • Meta (Facebook & Instagram): To manage posts, comments, and track engagement metrics.
  • TikTok: To analyze video performance and reach.
  • WhatsApp: To facilitate communication and automated messaging via the WhatsApp Business API.
  • Google: To retrieve advertising data and website traffic analytics.

3. Data Usage

We use the collected data strictly for providing cross-platform analytics, scheduling content, and generating AI-driven insights. We do not sell your data to third-party advertisers.

4. Security

We implement AES-256 encryption to secure your API tokens. Access is managed via OAuth 2.0; we never store your raw third-party passwords.
